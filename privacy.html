Privacy Policy

Effective date: 28 March 2026

LumaSync does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. LumaSync does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server.

Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device only, using the iOS Keychain and standard app storage:

  • Philips Hue bridge IP address and API credentials (stored in iOS Keychain)
  • TLS certificate fingerprint for your Hue bridge (stored in iOS Keychain)
  • Spatial map of light positions (stored in app's local Application Support directory)
  • Custom light show timelines you create (stored in app's local Application Support directory)
  • App preferences and settings (stored in UserDefaults)

None of this data ever leaves your device. It is not backed up to any external server controlled by us.

Permissions

LumaSync requests the following system permissions, used exclusively for the stated purpose:

  • Camera — used by ARKit and the LiDAR scanner to map the 3D position of your lights. Camera frames are processed in memory only; no images are saved or transmitted.
  • Microphone — used to analyse ambient audio in real time for the music sync feature. Audio is processed in memory only; no recordings are saved or transmitted.
  • Local Network — used to discover and communicate with your Philips Hue Bridge on your local Wi-Fi network. No data is sent to the internet.

Third-party services

LumaSync communicates only with your Philips Hue Bridge on your local network. During initial setup, a one-time request may be made to discovery.meethue.com (operated by Signify / Philips Hue) to locate your bridge IP address, if mDNS discovery fails. No personal data is included in this request.

No analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising networks, or other third-party trackers are included in the app.

Children's privacy

LumaSync does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date.
