Features

Intelligence built in.

Spatial Light Mapping

LiDAR scans your room and identifies each bulb in 3D space automatically — no manual positioning needed.

Live Music Sync

Real-time FFT audio analysis maps bass, mid, and treble to spatial light zones. Works with any music source.

Choreographed Shows

Twilight Cascade, Aurora Spectrum, Halloween Storm, and more — each timed to the millisecond at 50Hz.

Timeline Editor

Build your own shows with the keyframe editor. Define exactly when each light changes colour, temperature, or brightness.

50Hz Streaming

DTLS Entertainment API bypasses the standard REST rate limit. A full room of lights responds in under 20ms.

Full Room Control

Individual lights, groups, zones — all controllable with colour, temperature, and brightness.

Requirements

  • Philips Hue Bridge v2 or later (firmware 1.24+)
  • iPhone 12 Pro or later with LiDAR Scanner — required for spatial mapping
  • Or any iPad Pro (2020 or later)
  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • All other features (live sync, shows, manual control) work without LiDAR
